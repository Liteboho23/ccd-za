The National Identity

Celebrating Our Symbols and Heritage

Programme Focus: Promoting National Symbols, Holidays, and Civic Patriotism

"The National Identity" is a patriotic initiative aimed at fostering a strong sense of national pride and unity among South Africans. The programme seeks to deepen understanding of RSA’s national symbols, including the flag, coat of arms, national orders, and provincial emblems, and highlight their importance in representing our country’s identity. It encourages citizens to take pride in these symbols as a reflection of our shared heritage and values.

The programme also promotes respect and enthusiasm for RSA’s national holidays, such as Freedom Day and Heritage Day. It educates citizens on the historical and cultural significance of these days and encourages respectful participation in their celebrations. Through community activities, educational campaigns, and national events, the programme strives to instil patriotism, love, and passion for South Africa’s future, strengthening a unified national identity rooted in pride, respect, and shared history.
